I am Hayden Heroux. I am a student at Ponaganset High School studying in the Computer Science & Information Technology and Biomedical Science pathways. I started programming in sixth grade and it has been my obsession ever since.

My current project is a virtual computer modelled after the Commodore 64. It draws inspiration from a previous set of projects I worked on throughout 2020. I also have a number of tools, automations, and emulations on my Github.

In my free time I enjoy reading historical fantasy and non-fiction, and dreaming of elaborately engineered systems. I have a cat named Bengal and two turtles.
